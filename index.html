What is the sales floor image database Uribapedia?

It is a service that supports store promotion and package planning scenes with three features: "Distribution official store image download", "Speed ​​search system", and "Planning idea archive".

Distribution official store image

You can download the official store image of the retail company with which we research and cooperate. This is a royalty-free image with a license to the manufacturer planning department.

Speedy search system

You can search with free keywords like a major portal site, and you can search for images smoothly with a crisp search system (there is also a maniac detailed search function for over-the-counter marketers).

Planning ideas are also stocked

Utilizing the daily in-store coverage system and its knowledge, we have accumulated an archive of ideas for in-store promotion planning. When you are worried about the perspective of planning / proposal, you should be able to get a hint of ideas just by looking at it.

Not only the quantity but also the quality of information

Like a qualitative research company, we are building a database that focuses not only on the quantity of information but also on the quality. The information that is newly registered every day is only the latest in-store measures that have changed. We will spare no effort to improve the freshness and accuracy of such information.

Information features

The quantity of images is important, but at the same time, we are particular about the "quality" and "width" of information.

302,362

Number of recorded images

We regularly and regularly interview the sales floor and continue to stock images (since 2011). With a detailed coverage system that checks every corner of the store, we can swiftly catch even the slightest changes in the sales floor.

96,687,987

Number of stringed characters

A large amount of meta information (text information) is attached to each image data. As a result, we have realized a highly accessible search system that hits with various keywords according to the purpose.

More than half

Number of images discarded every month

You can't keep up with the changes in the sales floor just by taking pictures and collecting them. We maintain the "quality" of information by performing screening work that selects only new images from a large number of interview images. By the way, more than half of the images are discarded in the process.

SP Idea Archive

Specialized staff familiar with qualitative surveys pick up and strategically analyze "good" planning ideas found from daily sales floor observations and accumulate them as "sales floor topics". You can check sales promotion trends and planning ideas across industries.
